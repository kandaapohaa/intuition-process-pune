Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is intuition?▾
Intuition is the ability to understand or know something without relying on reasoning or logical analysis. It's naturally present in all of us, but children have easier access because their minds are fresh and more in tune with nature. This program nurtures and strengthens that innate ability.
What are the benefits?▾
Stronger intuition leads to improved learning ability, better decision-making, heightened creativity, enhanced problem-solving, stronger confidence, better interpersonal skills, and less fear of the unknown.
Is this online or in-person?▾
It's a hybrid program with both in-person weekend sessions and online weekday follow-ups via Zoom, supervised by expert instructors.
Which techniques are taught?▾
Children learn yogic techniques to relax the mind, age-appropriate breathing techniques, guided meditation, fun-learning games, and home practice instructions. Everything is safe, natural, and non-invasive.
How long to see results?▾
Each child is unique. The program encourages 15–25 minutes of daily home practice. With regular practice, parents have reported significant improvements in focus, confidence, and intuitive abilities.
Why only for children & teens?▾
Children's minds are still fresh, less obsessive, and naturally more in tune with nature — making it the ideal time to nurture and develop intuitive faculties.
