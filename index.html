Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is intuition?▾
Intuition is the ability to understand or know something without logical analysis. Children have easier access because their minds are fresh and in tune with nature. This program nurtures that innate ability.
What are the benefits?▾
Improved learning, better decisions, heightened creativity, enhanced problem-solving, stronger confidence, better interpersonal skills, and less fear of the unknown.
Online or in-person?▾
Hybrid — in-person weekend sessions plus online weekday follow-ups via Zoom, supervised by expert instructors.
Which techniques are taught?▾
Yogic relaxation techniques, age-appropriate breathing, guided meditation, fun games and activities, and home practice instructions. Safe, natural, non-invasive.
How long to see results?▾
Each child is unique. With 15–25 minutes of daily practice, parents report significant improvements in focus, confidence, and intuitive abilities.
Why only children & teens?▾
Children's minds are fresh, less obsessive, and naturally more in tune with nature — the ideal window to develop intuitive faculties.
